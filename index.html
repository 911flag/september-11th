The tragic events of 9/11 and its impact on the US and the world

September 11, 2001 was the tragic day marking the largest terrorist attack in US history when two passenger planes crashed into the twin towers of the World Trade Building in New York. This event caused thousands of casualties and tremendous damage, not only to Americans but also to the whole world. The horror and pain shook America, and the hearts of millions of people around the world sympathized with America at that time.
